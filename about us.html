About Us

Welcome to Chariots Coffees and Barista Academy! Our passion for coffee drives everything we do. From sourcing the finest beans from around the world to creating unique blends, we are committed to delivering an unparalleled coffee experience.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to share the love of coffee with everyone. Today, Chariots Coffees and Barista Academy stands as a hub for coffee enthusiasts, offering a wide range of products, educational content, and community-driven events.

Whether you?re a seasoned barista or just beginning your coffee journey, we are here to guide you every step of the way. Join us in celebrating the art of coffee making!

About Us

Chariots Coffee & Barista Academy was first registered in Uganda in 2018 as (A Single Member Company –SMC) under the Directorship of Mr. Simon Ochen Eidodo. But later in 2024 it acquired a new status as a private limited company upon admitting Mr. Gilbert Etomet as a co- director. Today, the company amongst its major goals, directly impacts lives through its academy which focuses on training young people into becoming seasonal Coffee Baristas and in recent years over 2000 young men and women have passed through the Academy successfully and are now spread all over the world excelling as Baristas amongst others. Additionally, Chariots Coffee and Barista Academy is an organization/ entity with comprehensive coffee business ideas, which combines a specialty coffee Roastery, café with a professional barista training school. Our mission is to provide high-quality coffee products to customers while offering barista training programs to aspiring coffee professionals. By integrating these three aspects in the coffee industry, we aim to create a unique and sustainable business model that caters for both coffee enthusiasts and industry professionals. .
